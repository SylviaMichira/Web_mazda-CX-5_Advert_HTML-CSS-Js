CX-5 Exterior and Performance

Captivating Design:

The Mazda CX-5 turns heads with its flowing body lines, signature wing grille, and sharp headlights featuring LED accents. Available premium features like 19-inch alloy wheels further enhance its sophisticated looks.

Powerful and Efficient Powertrains:

A choice of SKYACTIV-G gas engines deliver robust acceleration while maintaining excellent fuel economy. The available SKYACTIV-D diesel engine maximizes torque and efficiency.

Predictive i-ACTIV AWD {All-Wheel Drive}:

The available predictive i-ACTIV all-wheel drive system works proactively to distribute torque for optimum grip in all conditions. It enhances control on slippery surfaces.

Composed Handling and Ride:

The tuned suspension provides agile and responsive handling, while effectively absorbing bumps for a smooth comfortable ride. The CX-5 remains nimble and in control.
